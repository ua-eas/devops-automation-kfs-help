Overview

Reports

As delivered, the KFS provides access to a number of system generated reports for the accounts receivable, effort certification and general ledger modules. Reports are provided in various formats.

Reports submenu

Menu Group

Description

Accounts Receivable

Generates standard accounts receivable reports including billing statement, customer aging, and customer invoices derived from data within the KFS.

Effort Certification

Generates standard effort certification reports including duplicate certification report, effort certification extract build, outstanding certification by chart/org/report, and outstanding certification by report.

General Ledger

Generates a Trial Balance to validate that debits and credits balance in KFS.
