The document header for the Corporate Card Application document contains one travel-specific field in addition to the fields found in the standard document header.

Corporate Card Application status indicators

Application

Routes to the fiscal officer associated with the default account.

Routes to the Travel Manager role who will click the  to signify that a Corporate Card Application has been submitted to the bank.

Applied to Bank

Status of the document after the  has been clicked.

Approved by Bank

Status after the Travel Manager approves the document. The TEM Profile is updated with the corporate card information.
