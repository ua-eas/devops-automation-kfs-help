Title

 

Description

CAGE Number

Optional. Enter the Commercial And Government Entity, or CAGE, code associated with this Agency. This field is informational only and does not drive other fields in the KFS system.

DODAC Number

Optional. Enter the Department of Defense Activity Address Code (DoDAAC) that uniquely identifies a Department of Defense unit, activity, or organization that has the authority to requisition, contract for, receive, have custody of, issue, or ship DoD assets, or fund/pay bills for materials and/or services. This field is informational only and does not drive other fields in the KFS system.

DUN and Bradstreet Number

Optional. Enter the DUNS number associated with the agency. This field is informational only and does not drive other fields in the KFS system.

DUNS Plus Four Number

Optional. Enter the DUNS Plus Four Number associated with the DUNS number for this Agency. This field is informational only and does not drive other fields in the KFS system.
