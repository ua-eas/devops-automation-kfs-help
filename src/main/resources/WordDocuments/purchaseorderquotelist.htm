Purchase Order Quote List

 >  >  >  >

The Purchase Order Quote List document defines lists of vendors that have been created for a specific commodity. For example, a purchasing agent may need to get a quote for electrical supplies. Rather than add each electrical supply vendor to the quote one-by-one, he or she may select a pre-existing list of available electrical suppliers.
