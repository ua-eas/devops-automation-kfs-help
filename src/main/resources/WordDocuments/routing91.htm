Routing

•      The document routes to the fiscal officer associated with the default account used on the CTS Card Application.

•      After the Fiscal Officer approves, it routes to the Travel Manager.

•      Acknowledge requests are sent to the initiator when the Travel Manager clicks the  button and when the CTS Card Application is approved.
