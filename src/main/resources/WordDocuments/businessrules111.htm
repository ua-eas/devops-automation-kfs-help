Travel, Entertainment and Moving > Standard Transaction Documents > Travel Authorization Close > Process Overview
Business Rules

•      Travelers, arrangers and the Travel Manager may close a Travel Authorization that is Open for Reimbursement as long as one Travel Reimbursement has been generated against the Travel Authorization.

•      A Travel Authorization cannot be closed if its status is Held.

•      Travel Reimbursements can be processed against closed Travel Authorizations.
