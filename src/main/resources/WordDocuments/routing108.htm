Routing

•      Any active user may initiate an Equipment Loan/Return document.

•      The document is routed to the appropriate fiscal officer or delegate. The fiscal officer to whom this document is routed is defined by the account in the Asset Information tab of the document.

•      The document routes to the borrower ID.
