Predetmined Billing Schedule Tab

 

Predetermined Billing Schedule tab definition

Title

Description

 

 

Proposal Number

Required. An alphanumeric value that uniquely identifies the single proposal that the predetermined billing schedule will be associated with.  Use the lookup to find it.

 

Agency Name

Display only. The name of the agency to which the proposal belongs.

 

Total Award Amount

Display only. The total dollar amount calculated from Direct Cost Amount + Indirect Cost Amount for the award associated with the proposal.

 

Total Amount Scheduled

Display Only. The calculated total of all of the active bills listed on the bills tab.

 

Total Amount Remaining

Display only.  The calculated difference between the total award amount and the total amount scheduled.

 

Award Expiration Date

Display Only. The award stop date, for the award associated with the proposal and predetermined billing schedule.
