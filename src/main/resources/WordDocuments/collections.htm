'Collections'

In the Vendor document, many tabs allow you to enter multiple sets of information ('Collections') for the tab. For example, you may add multiple addresses, contacts, phone numbers, etc. for a single vendor.

To add additional sets of information, complete the tab again and click .

When a set of information is added it is collapsed but may be viewed by clicking  individually.
