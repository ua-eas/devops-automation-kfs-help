Labor Distribution > Labor Distribution Balance Inquiries

General Instructions

To access any of the balance inquiries and perform a search, follow these steps:

1.    Select the desired inquiry from the Labor Distribution submenu group in the Balance Inquiries menu group on the Main Menu tab.

The system displays the requested lookup screen. This screen may display default values; these values may be changed.

2.    Enter values in all fields preceded by an asterisk. Narrow the search as desired by including additional criteria.

3.    Click  when you have entered all necessary criteria and are ready to retrieve the balances.

4.    Drill down to the next level of detail.
