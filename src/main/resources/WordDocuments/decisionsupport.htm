Decision Support

Decision support is a collection of online services that provide access to the data within the KFS. Decision support includes inquiries and reports available in the KFS. You can use decision support to:

•      View account budgets, balances, and individual transactions.

•      Retrieve standard reports such as Account Status or Account Transaction Listing.

•      Generate pre-defined or custom reports from financial and system data.
