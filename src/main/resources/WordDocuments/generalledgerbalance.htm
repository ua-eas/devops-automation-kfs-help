General Ledger Balance

 > > > >

   For general information about entering lookup criteria, interpreting dollar accumulators, drilling down into inquiries to see more detail, and including or excluding pending entries, see GL Inquiries: Basic Information.

The General Ledger Balance inquiry shows an account's object code balances for each month of the fiscal year. Similar to some of the other KFS online inquiry screens the user can drill down into the balances to view transaction detail and individual documents.

The drilldown pattern of this inquiry is as follows:

1.    General Ledger Balance

2.    General Ledger Entry

3.    Document
