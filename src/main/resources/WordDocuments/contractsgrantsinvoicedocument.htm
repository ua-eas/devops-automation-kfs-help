Contracts & Grants Invoice Document

The Contracts & Grants Invoice document allows fund managers to edit, save, submit and approve invoices for contracts and grants activity to a customer, from your organization.  Depending on the invoicing option set on the award, this document creates one of three different invoice formats; Cost Reimbursable, Pre-Determined Billing Schedule or Milestone.
